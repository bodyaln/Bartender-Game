üéÆ How to Play

Objective

Your goal is to prepare cocktails according to given recipes by dragging ingredients into the glass in the correct order and quantity.

Basic Steps:

  1. Read the recipe - Check what you need to prepare
  2. Drag ingredients from the left panel to the glass
  3. Control pouring speed - Slow for precision, fast for quick pouring
  4. Rotate bottles when needed (for specific cocktails)
  5. Check your solution - Verify if the cocktail is correct
  6. Complete all 8 levels to finish the game
